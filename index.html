Tracking U.S. President Donald Trump’s tariffs globally

India caught in

Published: August 9, 2025

The U.S. President Donald Trump on August 6 signed an executive order bringing the effective tariff rate on India up to 50% - among the highest in the world. He had previously imposed a 25% tariff on India and both went into effect on August 7.

In reponse, Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi said Thursday welfare of farmers is of the “highest priority” for his government. “For us, the welfare of our farmers is of the highest priority. Bharat will never compromise on the interests of its farmers, dairy farmers, and fishermen. And I am fully aware that I may have to pay a very heavy price personally, but I am prepared for it. For the farmers of my country, for the fishermen of my country, for the dairy farmers of my country, Bharat stands ready today,” said Mr. Modi, inaugurating the M.S. Swaminathan Centenary International Conference.
